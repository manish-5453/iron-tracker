Why This Works

Progressive overload: Add weight or reps every 1–2 weeks.
RIR (Reps in Reserve): Stop sets with 1–2 reps left in the tank, not failure.
Volume: 10–20 sets per muscle per week is the proven hypertrophy zone.
Rest: 90–180s for compounds, 60–90s for isolation.
Sleep + surplus: Muscle is built outside the gym. 8h sleep + 300 kcal surplus.
